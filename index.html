Kickwise

A minimal kick counter that shows you the pattern.

Kickwise is a free, private kick-counting app for pregnancy. No ads, no accounts, no data collection — everything stays on your device (and your own private iCloud, if you turn sync on).

Need help?

Email feedback@kickwise.app and we'll get back to you.

Common questions

Is my data private?
Yes. Kickwise has no backend server, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs of any kind. Your sessions are stored on your device. If iCloud sync is on (default), they sync through your own private iCloud account — never through us, because we don't have a server to send them to. See the Privacy Policy for the full details.

Is Kickwise a medical device?
No. Kickwise is a tracking and discussion tool. It's not a substitute for professional medical advice — always follow your healthcare provider's guidance on fetal movement monitoring, and contact them directly with any concerns.

How do I turn off iCloud sync?
Settings → Data → iCloud sync.

How do I export my data?
Settings → Data → Export data, or Insights → Export PDF for doctor.
